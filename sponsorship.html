ASSC Sponsorship Packages

One Australian System Safety Conference sponsorship package is available. Pricing for the package is AUD $2,970 (GST inclusive). The deadline for sponsor registration is one week prior to the conference.

Sponsor package benefits:

  • One free full conference registration.
  • A 20% discount on three additional registrations.
  • The opportunity to provide a ten minute presentation.
  • The opportunity to display your company logo at the start of conference presentations.

Intending Sponsors need to request an invoice from secretary@ascsa.org.au. Sponsors need to provide delegate names and contact details as these need to be manually registered to obtain the free registration.
